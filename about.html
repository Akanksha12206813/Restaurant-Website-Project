About Us

Afra Tafri is a Cafe and Resturant designed to cater guests of all age groups.

Each savory dish by our chef is a perfect blend of fresh ingredients and suitable cooking methods. Prepared to be perfect accompaniments during meals, there are numerous refreshing mocktails to choose from.

A separate smoking area ensures patrons in the seating area have an undiluted dining experience. If you are visiting the resturant with your family, we will provide you seating in the dedicated family section. There's a banquet hall with a capacity of 150 guests suitable for kitty parties, engagements, birthdays, anneversaries and other celebrations. Pamper yourself with our complementary fish spa and leg massage services.

At Afra Tafri, we understand that visiting a resturant with your friends,family, or professional contacts is a way to strengthen your bond with them. This is why our chefs, attendants and management always strive to offer finest hospitality and wholesome food.
